IPO / New Issue Alert Concept Tier 3 · RM

A new IPO or sukuk issue matching the investor's own holdings opens. The subscription window is time-critical (a visual window cue), but subscribing to securities is a Tier-3 decision — so it routes to the advisor, never an in-app "subscribe." This is the only nudge in the set triggered by a market event, not the investor's balances or dates.

How IPOs and new issues work

An IPO (initial public offering) or new issue is the first time a company or fund offers securities to investors, during a set subscription window. Allocations aren't guaranteed, and the price can move once it lists. Because it involves securities, a licensed advisor reviews whether it suits your portfolio before you decide.

Why you're seeing this

What triggered it

A new issue opened in a sector and profile that matches holdings you already own — not every IPO, only ones that fit.

What it used

Only your own holdings data — nothing is shared outside the app.

How it's created

Our AI selects from a pre-approved library of nudges. It doesn't write investment advice or recommend you subscribe, and every nudge it sends is logged.

How often

You'll never get more than 2–5 nudges a week, counted across push, email and in-app.

You're in control

Mute or fine-tune any nudge category whenever you like.
