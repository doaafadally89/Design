What happens at maturity

When a structured deposit matures, your capital and any return are returned to your call account. Until reinvested, the proceeds earn the call-account rate — usually lower than a fresh term.

Why you're seeing this

What triggered it

Your structured deposit has matured and the proceeds aren't reinvested yet.

What it used

Only your own account data — nothing is shared outside the app.

How it's created

Our AI selects from a pre-approved library of nudges. It doesn't write investment advice, and every nudge it sends is logged.

How often

You'll never get more than 2–5 nudges a week, counted across push, email and in-app.

You're in control

Mute or fine-tune any nudge category whenever you like.

Try See my reinvestment options → Move to FD, or Remind me in a few days, then open the bell.
